# TetriXX — AI-Native Intelligence for Transport & Logistics Spend

> Automating Complexity, Delivering Clarity for a Sustainable Future

**TetriXX** is an AI-native intelligence company built for Fortune 500 
transport and logistics spend management. Headquartered in Singapore. 
Serving global enterprises across APAC and beyond.

---

## What We Build

**Freya** — our core platform — replaces legacy Freight Audit and Payment 
(FAP) systems with an AI-native agent layer that continuously verifies 
invoices, manages exceptions, and delivers structured spend intelligence 
across all transport modalities.

- Ocean · Air · Land · Courier — unified in one platform
- 98%+ audit accuracy in production
- Agentic exception resolution — autonomous, not manual
- MCP-native — interoperable with enterprise AI agent ecosystems

---

## The Problem We Solve

Large enterprises systematically overpay on freight because:

- Carrier invoices contain errors that rules-based systems miss
- Legacy FAP vendors (Trax, Cass, nVision, CTSI-Global) run on 
  30-year-old architecture with AI bolted on after the fact
- Manual audit teams are slow, expensive, and don't scale

Freya replaces all of this. Not a dashboard. A continuously operating 
intelligence system.
